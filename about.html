About me

My name is Daniel Vargas Castro. I was born on the 3rd of February, 1996 in La Paz, Baja California Sur, México.

In 2014, after finishing high school in my home town, I came to Mexico City aiming to become a computer engineer from the best university in the country: the National Autonomous University of Mexico. After taking the admission test twice, I can now call myself a "puma" still working for his dreams to become true.

I am currently in the seventh semester (out of nine) of my career. I am a full time student taking japanese lessons and also taking and imparting several extracurricular courses for the “Programa de Tecnología en Cómputo” (Computer Technology Program, PROTECO) where I am currently completing my social service. Sadly, this means I don't have much free time, but when I do, I like to hang out with my girlfriend or my friends, play videogames and play guitar.
